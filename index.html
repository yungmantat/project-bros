    About

    HOW IT ALL STARTED

    The Nespresso story began more than 30 years ago with a simple idea: enable anyone to create the perfect cup of espresso coffee - just like a skilled barista. From its beginning in 1986, Nespresso has redefined and revolutionised the way millions of people enjoy their espresso coffee and shaped the global coffee culture.

    A GLOBAL PRESENCE

    THE REST IS HISTORY

    For more than 30 years, our innovations have redefined the way millions of people enjoy their coffee, growing from a ground-breaking idea in Switzerland to a global brand with a presence in 81 countries. At the touch of a button, customers in homes, offices, hotels, restaurants and cafés can enjoy high-quality, sustainably produced coffee, cup after cup. Yes, we continue to grow and are still relentless in our focus on delivering sustainable quality at every stage of our value chain while at the same time maintaining a direct privileged relationship with our customers.

    FAQ
    How do I qualify for NESPRESSO & YOU?

    You are automatically enrolled into this program when you make a coffee purchase with your Nespresso account, directly from Nespresso Boutiques, Nespresso Online Boutique, Mobile App, or our Customer Service Hotline from 08:00am to 10:00pm.

    What is NESPRESSO & YOU?

    NESPRESSO & YOU is our new way of caring for you, with a series of benefits and rewards for all our members. Progress through the membership tiers to enjoy more benefits with us, either through your time with us (seniority) OR be accelerated up the tier by reaching the indicated consumption level.

    What are the maintenance tips when I first use my Vertuo machine?
    • Read the safety precautions carefully before using your new appliance for the first time. Keep them in a place where you can find and refer to them later on.
    • Please set the water hardness to soft (via connectivity) when first use or after factory reset.
    • Please re-fill the water tank with fresh portable water at room temperature. Do not use hot water.
    • Always lock the system completely and never open it during operation.
    • Please eject the capsule right after brewing each time.
    • Water may accumulate inside the machine’s head, please dry visible parts with a clean dry cloth.
    Why is the ring light on my machine blinking orange and white alternatively 3 times and then steady on?

    All our Vertuo coffee machines are equipped with a descaling alert. We recommend descaling your machine every 3 months or after 300 capsules, whichever comes first. Over time, water used during brewing may cause internal mineral build up, which can affect the flow, temperature and overall performance of your machine.

    I’m looking for a coffee machine for my office. Where can I buy one?

    You can find all of the various coffee machines for offices on our website. You can compare different models and choose the one that suits your company’s needs best. You can also request a demonstration online. If you need any additional information, you can complete the online form and send your request to us, or contact our Coffee Specialists by phone on 8101 1183.

    How often should I descale my Nespresso machine?

    In order to keep your Nespresso machine in optimal condition, you are advised to descale your machine after every 400 to 600 capsules or at least 2 times a year. The harder the water is in the area where you live, the more often you will need to descale your Nespresso machine. Water residue and limescale can accumulate within the system. This may not only affect the temperature, flow and quality of your espressos, but also shorten the life of your machine.

    I have purchased a descaling kit for another type of Nespresso machine. Can I use it anyway?

    The fluid in our descaling kits are identical, so you can use the set with another Nespresso machine as well. You must ensure, however, that you follow the appropriate descaling instructions for your machine.

    Can I bring my Nespresso machine abroad?

    The Nespresso machine range is specifically designed to meet precise power regulations for each geographical region. The machines sold for Hong Kong operate on 220-240V, 50/60Hz only. When using a machine with these specifications in another country, or vice versa, there is no guarantee that it will operate properly. Machines can be irreparably damaged when used in foreign countries.

    Can I trade in my machine?

    This program is currently not offered by Nespresso Hong Kong.

    My machine switches itself off after a while.

    Our machines are equipped with an ‘OFF mode’, which means that the machines turn themselves off when inactive to minimise energy use.
    Pixie/U/Inissia machines switch themselves OFF automatically after 9 min (factory settings). This time can be changed to 30 min.
